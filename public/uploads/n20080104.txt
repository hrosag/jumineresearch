BULLETIN TYPE:  Property-Asset or Share Disposition Agreement
BULLETIN DATE:  January 4, 2008
TSX Venture Tier 2 Company

TSX Venture Exchange has accepted for filing documentation in connection with 
two farm-in agreements entered into by Centric Energy Corp.'s wholly-owned 
subsidiary, Mali Oil Development SARL ("Mali Oil"), one with Heritage Mali 
Block 7 Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of Heritage Oil Corporation 
("Heritage"), a TSX listed issuer (the "Block 7 Farm-In Agreement") and one 
with Heritage Mali Block 11 Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of Heritage (the 
"Block 11 Farm-In Agreement"), each agreement dated November 17, 2007 
(together, the "Farm-In Agreements").

Under the Block 7 Farm-In Agreement, Mali Oil has sold a 75% participating 
interest in and to its Production Sharing Agreement with the Government of Mali 
respecting oil and gas exploration Block 7.  Under the Block 11 Farm-In 
Agreement, Mali Oil has sold a 75% participating interest in and to its 
Production Sharing Agreement with the Government of Mali respecting oil and gas 
exploration Block 11.

Under the terms of the Farm-In Agreements, the wholly owned subsidiaries of 
Heritage will earn the 75% interest in Blocks 7 and 11 in return for funding 
all costs of the obligatory work programs for the next two years in both 
Blocks. The obligations include the acquisition of 2D seismic data in both 
Blocks at a minimum cost of US$5 million, seismic and geological 
interpretation, and the drilling of one well to a depth of at least 2000 meters 
for a minimum cost of US$8 million.
